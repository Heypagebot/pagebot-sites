Our Story

Puttu. Reimagined.

Born in the heart of Kasaragod, The Puttu Project began with a single dream — to take Kerala's most iconic breakfast and dress it in flavours the world hadn't imagined yet.

From smoky beef to velvety cheese, from rich butter chicken to decadent chocolate, every puttu we serve is a conversation between tradition and adventure. And every meal is washed down with our signature unlimited Sulaimani — the tea that brings it all together.
