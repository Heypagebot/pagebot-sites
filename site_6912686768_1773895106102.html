Our Story

Puttu, reimagined for the bold.

The Puttu Project was born from a love for Kerala's humble steam-cooked classic and a hunger to push boundaries. We take the beloved puttu and transform it with rich, unexpected fillings — from succulent beef to silky butter chicken, smoky paneer to indulgent chocolate.

Every visit comes with our signature unlimited sulaimani — the perfect sip to complement every flavourful bite.
