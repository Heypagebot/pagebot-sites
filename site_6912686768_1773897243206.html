"Born from a love for Puttu"

What started as a dream in Kasaragod has become a celebration of Kerala's soul food. We took the humble puttu — the timeless steamed rice cylinder — and gave it a new story to tell.

Our Story

Puttu, Like You've
Never Tasted Before

At The Puttu Project, we believe tradition tastes even better with a twist. Our kitchen is a creative space where rice flour meets global flavors — buttery chicken gravies, smoky beef, melting cheese, rich paneer, and even indulgent chocolate.

Every plate is served with heart, and every visit comes with our signature perk: unlimited Sulaimani — the soul-warming black tea of Malabar — on the house.
