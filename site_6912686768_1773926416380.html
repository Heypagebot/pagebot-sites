Our Story

Born to Skate,
Built to Inspire

Hi, I'm Nevin — a 13-year-old skater from Kasaragod with a big dream: to build a skating community where everyone belongs. 🛼

Skating isn't just my hobby — it's my passion and purpose. I started young, fell a few times (literally!), but kept rolling because nothing beats the feeling of gliding freely with the wind in your face.

Now I'm on a mission to teach skating to children and adults, build confidence, and create a vibrant skating club for all enthusiasts in Kasaragod. Whether you're 5 or 50 — this club is for you! 💪
