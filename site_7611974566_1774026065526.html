Our Story

A Decade of Keeping
Mississauga Warm.

Peters Heating & Cooling was founded with one simple mission: to provide Mississauga homeowners with honest, expert HVAC service they can truly rely on. Over the past decade, we've grown from a one-truck operation to a trusted name across the city, built entirely on word-of-mouth and satisfied customers. We take pride in treating every home like our own and every client like a neighbor.

Our team is fully licensed, insured, and trained on the latest heating and cooling technologies. Whether it's a freezing January night or a sweltering August afternoon, we show up on time and get the job done right. We believe comfort shouldn't be a luxury — it should be a guarantee for every family in Mississauga.
