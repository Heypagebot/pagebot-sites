Our Story

More Than Just a Supermarket

Day Fresh Supermarket was born from a simple belief — every family deserves access to the freshest produce, quality groceries, and everything they need for home, all under one roof.

With three convenient branches in Vidyanagar, Cherkala, and Uliyathadka, we've grown close to the hearts of Kasaragod's communities. Our shelves are stocked with care, and our doors are open from early morning to late night — because we know life doesn't always wait.

From fresh vegetables picked daily, to warm bakery items, to every household essential you need — Day Fresh is your home away from home.
